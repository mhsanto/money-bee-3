3. The Purpose of Processing Your Personal Data

Your personal data is processed for legitimate and clearly defined purposes connected to our services. These include

Service Delivery & Application Processing:
  • To perform identity verification (eKYC) and due diligence checks as required by law.
  • To conduct a comprehensive assessment of your financial health, creditworthiness, and eligibility for financial products offered by our partners.
  • To facilitate the core function of our Service matching and submitting your application to our licensed lending partners for their consideration.
  • To enable our Case Managers to provide you with personalised guidance throughout the application process and to conduct post-drawdown support services.
Communication & Support:

  • To send you important notices, service updates, and changes to our terms, conditions, and policies.
Legal, Security & Compliance:
  • For the prevention, detection, and investigation of crime, including fraud and money laundering.
  • To comply with our legal and regulatory obligations under Malaysian law, including requirements under the Financial Services Act 2013 and the Anti-Money Laundering, Anti-Terrorism Financing and Proceeds of Unlawful Activities Act 2001 (AMLA).
  • To enforce our Terms and Conditions and protect our rights, property, and safety.
Business Improvement & Marketing:
  • For internal purposes such as auditing, data analysis, and research to improve our products, services, and AI algorithms. Where possible, this data will be anonymised and aggregated.
  • With your explicit consent, to send you marketing and promotional materials about new products or services that may be of interest to you. You may opt out of receiving these communications at any time.

4. Disclosure of Your Personal Data

We will not disclose your personal data to any third party without your consent, except to the following parties for the purposes listed above:

Our Licensed Lending Partners

Your completed application and supporting data will be shared with the potential lenders you are matched with, for the specific purpose of their credit assessment and loan approval process.

Third-Party Service Providers

We engage other companies to perform certain business-related functions (e.g., cloud hosting on secure servers like AWS, eKYC verification vendors, data analytics providers). These parties are provided only with the information necessary to perform their specific function and are bound by strict contractual confidentiality and data protection obligations.

Regulatory & Law Enforcement Agencies

We will disclose your data to parties such as Bank Negara Malaysia, PDRM, or other governmental authorities if we are required to do so by law or in the good faith belief that such action is necessary to comply with a legal obligation.

In the Event of a Corporate Transaction

If we are involved in a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, your Personal Data may be transferred as a business asset. We will provide notice before your Personal Data is transferred and becomes subject to a different Privacy Policy.

5. Security & International Transfer of Your Data

Our Security Measures

We have implemented comprehensive organisational and technical security measures to protect the integrity and confidentiality of your data. These include end-to-end SSL encryption, firewalls, intrusion detection systems, regular security audits, and strict internal access protocols and employee training on data protection.

International Transfers

As we may use internationally recognised cloud service providers, your data may be stored and processed on servers located outside of Malaysia. In such cases, we will ensure that the transfer is in compliance with the PDPA, and that the data is protected by contractual clauses or transferred only to jurisdictions that offer a comparable level of data protection.

6. Data Retention Policy

We will retain your personal data only for as long as is necessary to fulfil the purposes for which it was collected, or as required to comply with our business and legal obligations. In general, data related to financial transactions will be retained for a period of up to seven (7) years after the termination of your relationship with us, as required by law for audit and record-keeping purposes.

  • Licensed Credit Communities (Moneylenders) registered under the Moneylenders Act 1951 and regulated by the Ministry of Housing and Local Government (KPKT).
  • Digital Lending and P2P Platforms recognised and regulated by the Securities Commission Malaysia (SC).
